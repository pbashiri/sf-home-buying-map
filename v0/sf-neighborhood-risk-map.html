How this works

This isn't a scorecard. It shows real hazard geometry from the authoritative source for each layer, overlaid on the SF streetscape, so you can see whether a specific block or corridor is exposed.

Click anywhere on the map to see the concerns at that location: seismic, flood, upzoning, soft-story, school quality, walkability. Each concern includes the source and what you should do about it during diligence.

Known limits
  • Scored at the neighborhood level for most layers. Hazard polygons (liquefaction, flood, upzoning) are at real geometric resolution.
  • Soft-story is a per-building concern — always check DBI for the specific address.
  • GreatSchools ratings for dual-immersion / high-ELL schools underweight the experience of English-native families. Visit the schools.
  • SLR projections are scenarios, not forecasts. NASA 2023 InSAR suggests 2x subsidence on Bay fill.
  • This is not a substitute for a structural engineer, disclosure package, or real-estate lawyer.
